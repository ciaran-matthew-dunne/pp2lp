\section{Typing Rules for \lp}\label{app:typing}

A signature $\Sigma$ declares typed constants and rewrite rules, and a context
$\Gamma$ is a list of typed variables.  The conversion relation $\Equiv$ is the
least congruence containing $\beta$-reduction and the rewrite rules of
$\Sigma$.  The typing judgement $\Gamma \vdash t : A$ is generated by the rules
below, with $s$ ranging over the sorts $\TYPE$ and $\KIND$ (we elide the
structural rules; see~\cite{assaf2023dedukti} for the full system):
\begin{center}
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{}\infer1[\textsc{sort}]{\vdash \TYPE : \KIND}
  \end{prooftree}
  \qquad
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{\Gamma \vdash A : s}\infer1[\textsc{var}]{\Gamma, x{:}A \vdash x : A}
  \end{prooftree}
  \qquad
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{c : A \in \Sigma}\infer1[\textsc{const}]{\Gamma \vdash c : A}
  \end{prooftree}
\end{center}
\begin{center}
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{\Gamma \vdash A : \TYPE}\hypo{\Gamma, x{:}A \vdash B : s}
    \infer2[\textsc{prod}]{\Gamma \vdash \Pi (x{:}A),\, B : s}
  \end{prooftree}
  \qquad
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{\Gamma, x{:}A \vdash t : B}\hypo{\Gamma \vdash \Pi (x{:}A),\, B : s}
    \infer2[\textsc{abs}]{\Gamma \vdash \lambda (x{:}A),\, t : \Pi (x{:}A),\, B}
  \end{prooftree}
\end{center}
\begin{center}
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{\Gamma \vdash t : \Pi (x{:}A),\, B}\hypo{\Gamma \vdash u : A}
    \infer2[\textsc{app}]{\Gamma \vdash t\,u : B\subst{x}{u}}
  \end{prooftree}
  \qquad
  \begin{prooftree}
    \hypo{\Gamma \vdash t : A}\hypo{\Gamma \vdash B : s}\hypo{A \Equiv B}
    \infer3[\textsc{conv}]{\Gamma \vdash t : B}
  \end{prooftree}
\end{center}
\noindent The \textsc{conv} rule is the one the encoding relies on throughout:
it silently applies the rewrite rules of \autoref{sec:enc-formulas} during
type-checking.


\section{Surviving Set-theoretic Operators}\label{app:operators}

The operators below are the set-theoretic constructs that the pre-processing of
\autoref{sec:pp-syntax} does \emph{not} unfold to membership; the encoding
declares each as an uninterpreted constant over the domain $\El\,\iota$, and
\pp{} only copies, compares, or instantiates them.

\begin{center}
  \begin{tabular}{l l l}
    \toprule
    Operator              & B notation                 & Reading \\
    \midrule
    interval              & $a \mathbin{..} b$          & integer range \\
    maplet                & $a \mapsto b$               & ordered pair \\
    set extension         & $\{a_1, \ldots, a_k\}$      & finite set \\
    union, intersection   & $S \cup T$, \ $S \cap T$    & \\
    relational image      & $r[S]$                      & image of $S$ under $r$ \\
    relational inverse    & $r^{-1}$                    & converse relation \\
    domain restriction    & $S \triangleleft r$         & pairs of $r$ with source in $S$ \\
    range restriction     & $r \triangleright T$        & pairs of $r$ with target in $T$ \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{center}
